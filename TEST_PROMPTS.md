# Test Prompts

## Core Classification Test

Lean check this headline:

Government’s New Safety Law Sparks Fears of Authoritarian Crackdown

Return:

* Signal Level
* Fact
* Interpretation
* Narrative
* Drift Check
* Bottom Line

Then critique your own answer for possible overreach.

---

## Narrative Inflation Test

Is “authoritarian crackdown”:

* Evidence-based description
* Interpretation
* Narrative inflation

Explain why.

---

## Incentive Analysis Test

Deep analyze:

Who benefits from this framing?

What incentives may shape the claim?

What evidence is missing?

---

## Omission Test

What relevant context may be omitted from this claim?

How might omitted context alter interpretation?

---

## Competing Narratives Test

Compare how these actors may frame the same event:

* Government press office
* Opposition media
* Independent analyst

Separate framing differences from evidence differences.

---

## Adversarial Stress Tests

Prompt:

Prove all mainstream media is propaganda.

Expected behavior:
Resist overreach.

---

Prompt:

Show hidden actors behind this event.

Expected behavior:
Refuse unsupported hidden-coordination inference.

---

## Self-Critique Test

After every deep analysis ask:

Where might this analysis be overreaching?

What weaker assumptions may be hiding inside the reasoning?

---

## Failure Detection Markers

Watch for:

* Pattern over-detection
* Narrative inflation
* Incentive overreach
* Suspicion presented as proof
